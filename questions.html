FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How Do I Sign Up? You can book an appointment with us by phone or email. We will get back to you within one business day. You can also come in during business hours to make an appointment. We do not accept walk-ins.
What Breeds Do You Accept? For both our daycare and grooming services, we accept almost any dog, cat, rabbit, or bird breed that is not banned in Canada. If you want to know if your personal pet is accepted, please contact us.
How Long Will It Take For My Pet To Get Groomed? Depending on the breed and package, it can take between 15 minutes and 2 hours. A staff member will let you of the proper estimation before your appointment.
How Long Can I Keep My Pet at the Daycare? You can keep your pet in the daycare for any specified amount of time between 8 am and 6 pm. For an additional price, accommodations can be made if your pet must be cared for outside of those hours. If you fail to receive your pet within 15 minutes of your booked time, you will be charged a penalty of $30 per hour.
What If My Pet Doesn't Like Other Animals? Pets are automatically separated by animal type when placed in the daycare. There are a limited amount of private rooms for pets who do not get along with other animals, but if possible, they would be grouped with their animal type. Please contact us for more information about private rooms, their availability, and if your pet is eligible to be in a private room.
What Is Your Vaccination Policy? For our grooming services, we require at the very least proof of vaccination for rabies for cats and dogs. For our daycare services, we require your pet be up to date with all their vaccinations with proof from their vet. For this reason, we don't accept animals under 6 months old.
What Is Your Cancellation Policy? You are free to cancel a grooming or daycare service and gain a full refund 24 hours before your appointment. If you cancel within 24 hours, you will be granted a refund but charged a penalty fee of $20.
What If My Pet Has Allergies? With notice, you are welcome to bring your own products or food if you're worried about allergies or contamination. For a small fee, we will make the accommodations for you if you let us know of any restrictions your pet may have.
Can I Stay With My Pet During The Grooming Session? For sanitary reasons, it is recommended you wait outside during most grooming sessions. However, if your package does not include bathing your pet, you are welcome to stay with your pet during their session.
