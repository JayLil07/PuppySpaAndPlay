GROOMING SERVICE

About Our Grooming Service

With our grooming services, we aim to give every animal a comfortable and clean life. We offer a variety of different services ranging from basic cleaning to extensive treatments to fit your lifestyle and situation. All your grooming needs are right here, conveniently located in one place! Please note the rabbit and bird prices may be slightly higher. This is due to the rare nature of grooming such pets.

Nail Trimming
Basic nail trimming and paw care. This includes trimming overgrown bird beaks (if applicable)
Brush Out
Basic brushing of fur and application of oil to keep your pets fur in good condition. This is a good option if you want to maintain your pets shiny fur.
Simple Bath
A basic bath with soap and conditioner. Emphasis on washing the pet's face.
Everything Bath
An in-depth cleaning containing a thorough brushing and dematting, cleaning ears, face, and paws, a light fur trim, and a nice bath.
Fur Trimming
Trimming fur to detangle it or to prepare for hot summers.
Fur Shaving
Shaving your pet down to the skin. This can be done for a variety of reasons, including matting, medicinal reasons, or preference.
Extensive Fur Treatment
For pets with severly mistreated fur, this treatment tackles everything and has your pet coming out cleaner and shiner than ever!
Flea Removal
An extensive treatment to remove fleas. We would also recommend aquiring flea medication to use after this treatment.
